Reuben Sandwich

An Easy Reuben

This is going to be a simple recipe that you can make out of what may
seem like the most random of ingredients but when combined makes a
savory and elegant sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Rye bread
  • Sauerkraut
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Corned Beef
  • Thousand Island
    Dressing

Instructions:

  1. Start by preheating your pan to medium and taking out
    about 4 slices of corned beef. Lay the corned beef in the
    pan and let cook until it starts to curl and flip over.

  2. While corned beef is cooking, place sauerkraut in
    medium saucepan over medium-low heat.

  3. When corned beef starts to curl, place two slices of
    rye bread in the toaster to desired doneness.

  4. Please two slices of swiss cheese over corned beef in
    pan and cover til melted.

  5. Take corned beef with melted swiss out of the pan and
    carefully place onto one slice of rye bread, careful to make
    sure cheese is evenly spread across.

  6. Remove sauerkraut from heat and scoop out liberally over
    over the corned beef. Add liberal amount of Thousand Island
    and let rest for about 3 minutes before eating.
